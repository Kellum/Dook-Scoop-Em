## Challenges & Obstacles

**Getting customers from curious to committed:** People browsing local service websites often leave without booking because the process feels complicated or they're not sure they're in the service area. Every extra question or confusing step means lost customers.

**Handling the money side reliably:** A subscription business lives or dies on billing working correctly every month. Tracking who paid, who canceled, and who needs a reminder had to be bulletproof. Small businesses can't afford payment errors or missed charges.

**Making it simple enough to run solo:** The business owner can't spend hours per day managing a complicated system. Between servicing yards and answering phones, they need to check the schedule, see new signups, and track revenue in under five minutes.

**Mobile experience matters more than desktop:** Most customers make buying decisions from their phone while standing in the yard with their dog. If the site is clunky on mobile or the signup process doesn't work smoothly on a small screen, they'll give up and call a competitor.

**Service areas keep expanding:** As the business grows, new Jacksonville neighborhoods get added to the coverage map. Manually tracking which zip codes are active and turning away customers from areas not yet served had to be flexible and easy to update.

---

## Solutions Implemented

**Streamlined booking flow:** We built a step-by-step signup process that asks one question at a time so it never feels overwhelming. The system checks the customer's zip code immediately and only shows pricing for service plans available in their area. If they're outside the coverage zone, they can join a waitlist instead of just getting rejected.

**Automated payment system:** All billing runs through Stripe, which handles the heavy lifting of charging cards, sending receipts, and managing subscriptions. When a customer signs up, their payment processes immediately, and the system automatically charges them every month. The business owner never touches credit card numbers or worries about security compliance.

**Built-in business dashboard:** The owner logs into an admin view that shows today's visits, total monthly revenue, and a list of every customer with their contact info and subscription status. Finding a customer's gate code or checking when they signed up takes seconds. New quote requests from the website appear immediately so the owner can follow up while the lead is hot.

**Mobile-first design:** Every page adapts perfectly to phone screens with big, easy-to-tap buttons and forms designed for thumbs. The booking process works smoothly on a phone screen, and customers can log in from anywhere to manage their accountâ€”no need to wait until they're at a computer.

**Flexible service area management:** Adding a new neighborhood to the coverage map takes one click in the admin dashboard. When customers enter a zip code during signup, the system instantly checks if service is available there or puts them on a waitlist for future expansion. As the business grows across Jacksonville, the website keeps up without needing a developer.
